This website allows users to gather information about H1b-Visa administered by the US government.
H1b-Visa is a work visa for non-citizens and non-permanent residents. Information conveyed by this program include the initial approvals, continual approvals, initial denials and continual denials for approximately 50,000 companies that operate within the US.
